French
Quarter
Festival

April 9–12, 2015

The award-winning French Quarter Festival is a four-day local music showcase scattered throughout the French Quarter in New Orleans, Louisiana—the largest free music event in the United States. French Quarter Festival features 400 hours of free, local music from a variety of musical genres on over 20 stages throughout the French Quarter. The community festival also features the “World’s Largest Jazz Brunch”, festival food booths operated by some of New Orleans finest restaurants, open throughout the festival weekend.
